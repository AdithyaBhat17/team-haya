About Us

TEAM HAYA is the automotive club of PES University which specializes in the design and fabrication of All Terrain Vehicles. The team was started by a small group of passionate students and today has 40+ members. In order to produce a top notch car every year the team operates like an ethical professional engineering force. The team is currently in its fourth year and takes part in national events like ENDURO STUDENT INDIA and BAJA SAE INDIA. The team provides a platform for its members to understand and overcome real world challenges associated with the automotive industry like vehicular design, manufacturing, dynamic analysis and marketing.

About Events

BAJA SAE INDIA

BAJA was started in India in the year 2007 by SAE India. Every year it is held in NATRAX, Pithampur, Indore.

BAJA SAE INDIA

ESI was started in the year 2014 and every year it is held at GEDEE race track in Coimbatore and in 2019, it was held in Pune, India.

BAJA SAE INDIA

This is the formula student event conducted at Buddh International circuit, Noida.

BAJA SAE INDIA

This is the event conducted by the Indian leg of the global formula student organization. It is set in Kari motor speed way, Coimbatore.
